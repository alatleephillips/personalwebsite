Poster Templates

Probably the most common request I get from peers is a template for making posters using Affinity Designer. I used to send my old posters just as is, but then I decided to make some more customizable templates! Each template is inspired by a photograph, which is where I draw a lot of my inspiration from for color schemes. For each scheme developed, there are three options: the photo as a banner, the photo as the background, and one without the photograph at all. Enjoy!
