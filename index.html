About Me

Hey! I’m Alex and I’m a scientist, photographer, graphic designer, science communication/science policy nerd, DEI advocate, teacher, and generally really into doing too many things at once. I’ve tried to compile my ever-growing hobbies into this website. Don’t hesitate to get in touch - whether it’s a question about graduate school, a comment about my publications, a discussion on outreach, or even a particularly great cookie recipe, I’m here for it!
